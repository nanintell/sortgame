Design Strategy


For Sorting Algorithm, we mostly use 2 Design Strategies, as shown below.
  1. Decrease and Conquer: The problem is split into 2 parts, solved part and unsolved part then we keep expanding solved part until there isn't any unsolved part left
  2. Divide and Conquer: The problem is cut into half then we keep cutting it until it can no longer be cut. After that, we solve each cut parts and merge those solved ones into one solved problem.
